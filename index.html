About Us

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Akalakunnam village is 628160. Akalakunnam village is located in Kottayam taluka of Kottayam district in Kerala, India. It is situated 26km away from Kottayam, which is both district & sub-district headquarter of Akalakunnam village. As per 2009 stats, Akalakunnam village is also a gram panchayat. When it comes to administration, Akalakunnam village is administrated by a sarpanch who is elected representative of the village by the local elections. As per 2019 stats, Akalakunnam village comes under Puthuppally assembly constituency & Kottayam parliamentary constituency. Palai is nearest town to akalakunnam for all major economic activities, which is approximately 16km away.

  • The total geographical area of village is 2357 hectares. Akalakunnam has a total population of 15,082 peoples, out of which male population is 7,379 while female population is 7,703. Literacy rate of akalakunnam village is 89.64% out of which 90.16% males and 89.15% females are literate. There are about 3,712 houses in akalakunnam village. Pincode of akalakunnam village locality is 686504.
